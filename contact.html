Frequently Asked Questions

Is Beat by Beat's information medically accurate?

Yes, all our content is based on current medical guidelines and best practices. However, we always recommend taking formal first aid courses for hands-on training and certification.

Can I use Beat by Beat materials for teaching?

Absolutely! Our educational resources are designed to be shared. We encourage teachers, trainers, and community leaders to use our materials to educate others.

How often is content updated?

We regularly review and update our content to ensure it reflects the latest medical guidelines and best practices in emergency response.

Do you offer certification courses?

Currently, Beat by Beat provides educational resources only. For official certification, we recommend contacting local Red Cross chapters or certified training organizations.

How can I suggest new topics?

We welcome suggestions! Email us at info@beatbybeat.org with your topic ideas. We prioritize content based on community needs and safety impact.
